WAYS TO GIVE

Cryptocurrency


Give Cryptocurrency

Be a hero for pets by donating your Bitcoin (BTC), Ether (ETH), Litecoin (LTC), Bitcoin Cash (BCH), Z-cash (ZEC), Filecoin (FIL), Dogecoin (DOGE), or any tokens currently accepted on the Gemini Exchange. BISSELL Pet Foundation is committed to making the largest possible impact to help every pet find a home. Your cryptocurrency donation will help us achieve our goal.

 

Cryptocurrencies are classified as stocks by the IRS. Donating cryptocurrency is a non-taxable event. Learn more about donating crypto by contacting your tax or financial advisor for more or by visiting thegivingblock.com/faq.
